---

sentinel is a self-hosted personal finance system. It brings your investments, expenses, and tax planning together in one dashboard. It acts as a replacement for Koinly, Mint, and Empower. This software lets you control your data and view all financial information in one place. It works on Windows without needing technical skills.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

- Windows 10 or later (64-bit recommended)
- 4 GB of RAM minimum; 8 GB suggested for best performance
- At least 500 MB of free disk space
- Internet connection for initial download and updates

The application runs locally on your PC. It uses a lightweight database to store your data privately on your machine.

## ⚙️ Initial Setup

When you run sentinel for the first time, the app will guide you through basic setup.

1. You will be asked to create a user profile. This profile stores your financial data.
2. Connect your financial accounts. This process uses standard login screens for banks or investing platforms. The app does not store your passwords.
3. Set your preferred currency and tax region. This helps the app calculate tax strategy according to your location.
4. Import any existing data from CSV files if available. This is optional.

The UI is straightforward. You will see three main sections: investments, expenses, and tax overview.
